Responsive

Around 52% of website traffic is from mobile devices and that percentage goes up every year. So one of my highest priorities is ensuring that a website is fully responsive and will run on all devices.

Speed

Walmart found that for every second of load time improvement they experienced a 2% conversion increase, and Google that an extra 0.5 seconds for searches caused traffic to drop 20%. So the loading time and overall speed of a website is important to not only ensure a consistent user experience across all devices but also increase user conversion.

Design

Good web design ties into both of the previous points and can also reflect on your business or organization. A well designed and easy to use website is crucial to ensure that your website achieves its goals.
